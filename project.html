M A M A M O O

Mamamoo is a girl group member established by RBW on the year 2014. They are recognized to be one of the best because of their achievements just for a short span of time after debuting. Their fandom is called MooMoo, inspired by a korean radish. They are known to be one of the best vocal in K-pop industry, they have great harmonization and they can kick-off in dancing as well. Not just only performers but also models from different product brands, from food, clothes and even appliances.

MEMBER'S PROFILE

Moon Byul Yi

(Moonbyul)

  • Main Rapper, Performer, Vocalist, Visual
  • Born on December 22, 1992
  • She was born in Bucheon, South Korea
  • Moonbyul had originally auditioned to be a vocalist but changed to become a rapper instead
  • When MAMAMOO participated to Immortal Songs, she wrote the rap parts by herself as they were not originally there
  • Moonbyul has 4 tattoos
  • Moonbyul’s ideal type: “I really like Gong Yoo sunbaenim. I sort of like dinosaur face-types and guys with broad shoulders, guys who can just grab and hug me in one go”.
This is the logo of Moonbyul as a solo artist. Whenever she has a solo projects, she will promote with this logo. Red is the official color of Moonbyul

Kim Yong Sun

(Solar)

  • Leader, Main Vocalist, Visual
  • Born on February 21, 1991
  • She was born in Gangseo-gu, Seoul, South Korea.
  • She considers herself Mamamoo’s Mom
  • Solar created the official fan song for moomoos
  • Solar is the only one in the group with no tattoos
  • Moonbyul’s ideal type: “I like Simon D sunbaenim. I like hip-hop and when he raps, he’s so impressive and cool”.
This is the logo of Solar as a solo artist. Whenever she has a solo projects, she will promote with this logo. Blue is the official color of Moonbyul

Jung Whee In

(Wheein)

  • Lead Vocalist, Performer
  • April 17, 1995
  • She was born in Jeonju, Jeollabuk-do, South Korea
  • Likes watching mokbang shows (eating shows)
  • She likes to draw
  • Wheein has 6 tattoos
  • Moonbyul’s ideal type: “I like Beenzino sunbaenim, but I mean he’s basically the ideal type for all girls in their 20s”.
This is the logo of Wheein as a solo artist. Whenever she has a solo projects, she will promote with this logo. White is the official color of Moonbyul

Ahn Hye Jin

(Hwasa)

  • Lead Vocalist, Lead Rapper, Maknae
  • Born on July 23, 1995
  • She was born in Jeonju, Jeollabuk-do, South Korea
  • Has known Wheein since middle school
  • Wrote and Composed “My Heart/I Do Me” on Mamamoo’s first mini-album, “Hello”
  • Hwasa has 5 tattoos (Wheein and Hwasa share two friendship tattoos)
  • On February 13th 2019, Hwasa released her first solo song called TWIT
  • Moonbyul’s ideal type: “I like guys who are fatherly so, George Clooney is my ideal type”.
This is the logo of Hwasa as a solo artist. Whenever she has a solo projects, she will promote with this logo. Yellow is the official color of Moonbyul
