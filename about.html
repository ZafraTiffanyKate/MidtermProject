ABOUT THE PAGE

This web page ought to entertain people who are a fan of K-pop group, Mamamoo! The objective of this web page is to gather more supporters around the world and to have interaction between fans from different countries by following Mamamoo's social media accounts as well as keep them updated.

First slide

Mamamoo is a girl group member established by RBW on the year 2014. They are recognized to be one of the best because of their achievements just for a short span of time after debuting.

Second slide

Their fandom is called MooMoo, inspired by a korean radish. They are known to be one of the best vocal in K-pop industry, they have great harmonization and they can kick-off in dancing as well.

Third slide

Not just only performers but also models from different product brands, from food, clothes and even appliances. The products that they are endorsing will be sold out anywhere in South Korea, that's how influencial they are to the people.
